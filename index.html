Global Migration Overview
in 2020

This aims to visualise migration statistics over the years in different countries as well as the relationship between the number of migrants and factors such as age group, gender and economy. The purpose of this simple webpage is for FIT3179 Homework Week 10.

Migrations By Country and GDP

We can see that the United States of America , China and India has the highest number of incoming migrants. An interesting finding is that although India has a much lower GDP Per Capita compared to China, they had 1,380,004 migrants in 2020, which is only ~60,000 lower compared to 1,439,324 migrants in China.
